DEEP SDR

Software-defined radio (SDR) combined with a USB receiver is an easy way to make your PC receive radio waves. Since everything happens in software, we can add deep processing (aka AI: LLMs, ML transcription) and make an incident map. I describe a project that does that in my YouTube video.
